Introducing the First-Ever 24-Hour Trade and Investment Fair

Message from the Volta Regional Minister

The Volta Region stands ready to welcome the world. With its breathtaking natural beauty, rich tapestry of traditions, and a rapidly evolving business landscape, Volta is more than a region — it is a thriving hub of opportunity waiting to be explored. As we prepare to host the 7th Volta Trade and Investment Fair, we extend a warm invitation to exhibitors, investors, entrepreneurs, and tourists from across Ghana and beyond. This year’s fair is more than just an exhibition — it is a vibrant platform designed to connect visionaries, inspire innovation, and ignite lasting partnerships. Discover boundless opportunities in Volta — where potential meets promise. From our fertile lands that offer endless agricultural potential to our bustling markets and emerging industries, the Volta Region provides a dynamic environment for investment and growth. The fair serves as a gateway, opening doors not only to Volta but also to the vast markets to the east, including Togo, Benin, Nigeria, and even as far as Cameroon. Come and experience the warmth and hospitality of our people, taste our unique and flavorful cuisine, explore our pristine landscapes, and witness firsthand the creative spirit that defines Volta. Visit Volta. Taste Volta. Invest in Volta. Join us in shaping the future of trade and investment in West Africa — and be part of a story of growth, connection, and transformation.

About us

Rooted in community and focused on growth, the Fair bridges local potential with global opportunities. We empower businesses, foster collaborations, and highlight the Volta Region as a hub of trade, culture, and innovation. Join us to experience enterprise with purpose.
